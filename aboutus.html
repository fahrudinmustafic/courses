Our Academy

Akademy is an educational center whose scope of work in the field of education significantly increases year after year of active commitment to providing practical level of knowledge that students acquire in our centers, regardless of their decision about the type of a course, vocational or additional trainings that you can complete. We take responsibility to satisfy our students with our approach to the training process and teaching as a measure of our quality of work. With this kind of attitude of self-assessment, we are a team that encounters a standard good response from students who recognize our seriousness in our standardized courses and obtaining valid certificates and diplomas. Meeting the needs and demands of our student, related to different areas of education, is a part of our research work that aims not only to have active perception of the current demand in the market because we are always motivated by a vision and desire to get ahead; therefore we are innovators and the first who offer more and better than what the market offers a certain point of time.

We have demonstrated our responsibility since the very beginning of our Foreign Languages School; therefore our courses are fully aligned to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). All our language courses are divided into levels and follow CEF requirements. Complying with standards is always a guarantee of quality. At our lectures, standards prescribed by the aforementioned reference frame are respected and followed, and we insist on interactive approach as the most distinguished method today. With vibrancy and dynamics in our classes as a big plus, everybody gets the best results. You cannot learn a language if you are silent, therefore in our classes we provide real conversations. Different discussion topics are always chosen according to the needs and requirements of our students. That is an additional aspect of our work that we are proud of.
